Our Mission

PawHaven connects rescued animals with loving homes by coordinating rescuers, transporters, and adopters in one place. We believe every pet, regardless of age, breed, or condition, deserves a safe and caring home.

Our Story

PawHaven began as a small coordination tool for local rescue volunteers struggling to track adoption status across spreadsheets and group chats. Today it's grown into a full platform connecting rescuers, transporters, and adopters.

Our Values

Transparency, urgency, and compassion guide every feature we build — from real-time status tracking to verified pickup and drop-off confirmations for every rescue case.

Our Community

We work alongside independent rescuers, local shelters, and volunteer transporters who give their time to make sure no animal is left behind.

Our Goal

To make the adoption process faster, more transparent, and less stressful — for both the animals waiting for homes and the people trying to help them.
